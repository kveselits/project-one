Short Bio:

lan Turing is often called the father of modern computing. He was a brilliant mathematician and logician. He developed the idea of the modern computer and artificial intelligence. During the Second World War he worked for the government breaking the enemies codes and Churchill said he shortened the war by two years.

Notable Achievements:

  1. Cracked the Enigma Code during World War 2.
  2. Created the Turing Test to determine whether a machine was sentient.
  3. His theories led to the creation of computers.
